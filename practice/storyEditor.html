Instructions

  • To Save: Enter a story title and Click Save
  • To Edit: Enter a story title in the story name field and click Find and Edit (note: it must be an already saved story title.) When you are done editing click Save my Story
  • To View: Write a new story or load a saved one into the editor, than click Display my story. It will be displayed below the text editing field.
