Instructions

Welcome to My Heroes. Here you can view, add, or remove a hero from your own personal collection. The only requirement to be a hero is to have an alias. *Note Your heroes will be reset if you reload the page

To add a hero: click the Add new Hero button, fill in the required info and click submit.

To remove a hero: click the Remove Hero button, fill in the hero's alias to be removed and click submit

To view or hide the list of your heroes click the Display my Heroes button
